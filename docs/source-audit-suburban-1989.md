# Chevrolet Suburban 1989 exterior-color source audit

## Decision

The official 1989 GM Heritage Vehicle Information Kit does not contain a
governing solid or same-code primary exterior-color chart. PDF pages 18–20,
printed pages 21–23, contain only Conventional Two-Tone (ZY2), Special
Two-Tone (ZY3), and Deluxe Two-Tone (ZY4) combination tables. None contains a
same-code solid row. This kit therefore does not itself establish standalone
availability, and no scheme component is promoted. A separate model-specific
GM-authored sales brochure, audited in the
[1982, 1989, and 1993 regular-palette gap audit](source-audit-suburban-1982-1989-1993.md),
supplies the ten published 1989 regular colors.

The three pages still provide exact paint-scheme evidence. ZY2 contains 48
printed pairs. ZY3 and ZY4 each contain 70 printed rows across the same 48
unique pairs. The normalized paint-scheme tables retain all 188 printed rows.

## Verification

- The retained PDF has 21 pages, is 1,042,568 bytes, and has SHA-256
  `a908a97a41c32a429a1cf2eac9de9a7ec3383d4bdbbd1c3daa02cde8c3b9ed2d`.
- The artifact ledger records source ID
  `gm-heritage-1989-chevrolet-suburban`, HTTP 200, and `application/pdf`.
- PDF pages 18–20 were rendered at 400 DPI and checked row by row.
- The page counts reconcile to 48 ZY2 rows, 70 ZY3 rows, and 70 ZY4 rows.
- No ZY0 chart appears in the 1987, 1988, or 1989 Suburban kits.
