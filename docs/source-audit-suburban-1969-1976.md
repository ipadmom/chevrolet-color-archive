# Chevrolet Suburban 1963 and 1969-1976 color-source audit

## Result

Official, model-specific Chevrolet Suburban charts establish complete solid-color palettes for 1969 and 1972 through 1976. The official 1963, 1970, and 1971 Suburban kits refer to color sections that are absent from the scanned documents. Those three years are reviewed-no-chart records: their official kits were completely reviewed, but zero color rows are published. General truck, Blazer, refinish-library, and retailer records remain comparison evidence only.

| Model year | Result | Governing locator | Published solid colors |
|---|---|---|---:|
| 1963 | Reviewed, no chart | Dedicated kit PDF pages 8-9 refer to an absent Colors section | 0 |
| 1969 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF page 10; PDF page 30 directs Suburban paint orders to the chart | 15 |
| 1970 | Reviewed, no chart | Dedicated kit PDF page 6 refers to an absent Color and Trim chart | 0 |
| 1971 | Reviewed, no chart | Dedicated kit PDF page 8 refers to an absent chart; all 17 pages checked | 0 |
| 1972 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF page 18, printed page 17; diagrams on PDF page 19 | 15 |
| 1973 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF page 27, printed page 26; diagrams on PDF page 28 | 15 |
| 1974 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF page 11, printed page 10; diagrams on PDF page 12 | 15 |
| 1975 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF pages 6-7 | 15 |
| 1976 | Complete | Dedicated kit PDF pages 6-7; chart dated January 16, 1976 | 15 |

## Explicit no-chart years and supplemental leads

### 1963

Governing official Suburban source:

- `gm-heritage-1963-chevrolet-suburban`: the [dedicated 1963 Suburban kit](https://www.gm.com/content/dam/company/no_search/heritage-archive-docs/vehicle-information-kits/chevrolet/1963-Chevrolet-Suburban.pdf), also preserved as a [retained Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1963-chevrolet-suburban-vehicle-information-kit-gm.pdf). PDF page 8 identifies C1406/Carryall; PDF page 9 says `Paint, Exterior: See Colors section.` The section is absent. This is the source of the reviewed-no-chart finding.

Comparison sources, not Suburban availability proof:

- `gm-heritage-1963-chevrolet-truck`: the [complete 1963 Chevrolet Truck kit](https://www.gm.com/content/dam/company/no_search/heritage-archive-docs/vehicle-information-kits/chevrolet-trucks/1963-Chevrolet-Truck.pdf), preserved as a [retained Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1963-chevrolet-truck-vehicle-information-kit-gm.pdf). All 107 pages were checked. Pickup model sheets repeatedly refer to a Colors section, but no chart, chips, names, or codes appear.
- `chevrolet-service-news-1963-truck-refinish-extract`: the exact [two-page Chevrolet Service News truck refinish extract](http://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/resources/63-PntRefCodes.pdf), recovered through its [December 15, 2017 Internet Archive capture](https://web.archive.org/web/20171215004756id_/http://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/resources/63-PntRefCodes.pdf) and preserved as a [retained Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1963-chevrolet-service-news-paint-refinish-codes.pdf). It is a general Chevrolet Truck and Corvair 95 refinish reference.
- `chevrolet-service-news-volume-34-no-9`: the complete [Chevrolet Service News, volume 34, number 9, October 1962](https://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/documents/manuals/ChevServNws/CSN-34-9.pdf), recovered through its [October 17, 2022 Internet Archive capture](https://web.archive.org/web/20221017094958id_/https://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/documents/manuals/ChevServNws/CSN-34-9.pdf) and preserved as a [retained 48-page Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1962-october-chevrolet-service-news-vol-34-no-9.pdf). It is the complete parent issue for the two-page extract.
- `corvanatics-corvair95-paint-trim-refinish-codes`: the [Corvair 95 paint, trim, and Chevrolet Service News refinish-code compilation](https://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/resources/PainTrimRefinCodes.pdf), recovered through its [May 19, 2024 Internet Archive capture](https://web.archive.org/web/20240519115026id_/https://www.corvair.org/chapters/corvanatics/files/resources/PainTrimRefinCodes.pdf) and preserved as a [retained 11-page Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/corvair95-paint-trim-refinish-codes.pdf). Its 1963 pages corroborate the general refinish list but do not establish Suburban-specific availability.

### 1971

Governing official Suburban source:

- `gm-heritage-1971-chevrolet-suburban`: the [dedicated 1971 Suburban kit](https://www.gm.com/content/dam/company/no_search/heritage-archive-docs/vehicle-information-kits/chevrolet/1971-Chevrolet-Suburban.pdf), also preserved as a [retained Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1971-chevrolet-suburban-vehicle-information-kit-gm.pdf). Comparison source, not Suburban availability proof:

- `gm-heritage-1971-chevrolet-blazer`: the [1971 Chevrolet Blazer and full-size recreational-vehicle information kit](https://www.gm.com/content/dam/company/no_search/heritage-archive-docs/vehicle-information-kits/chevrolet/1971-Chevrolet-Blazer_RV_Full-Size.pdf), preserved as a [retained Release PDF](https://github.com/ipadmom/chevrolet-color-archive/releases/download/brochure-source-archive-v1/1971-chevrolet-blazer-rv-full-size-vehicle-information-kit-gm.pdf). PDF page 63 contains a Blazer-specific palette, not Suburban proof.

RockAuto, service-refinish catalogs, and general truck charts may supply search leads or aliases. They do not establish that Chevrolet offered a paint on a Suburban model year. Promotion requires a factory model chart or equivalent model-specific manufacturer document.

The [GM Heritage Archive](https://www.gm.com/heritage/archive) invites research requests. The omitted 1963, 1970, and 1971 sheets are candidates for a future archive request; no request was sent during this audit.
