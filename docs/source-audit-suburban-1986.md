# Chevrolet Suburban 1986 exterior-color source audit

## Decision

The official 1986 GM Heritage Vehicle Information Kit contains the C/K Suburban
“Interior and Exterior Color Availability Chart” at PDF page 32, printed C/K
Suburban Page H. The scan is rotated and trims the chart’s outer title and date.
The official 1985 kit contains a complete duplicate at PDF page 67 dated
September 1985. The row content matches. The public solid-color timeline
publishes the ten primary colors from that revision.

The earlier August 1984 chart at PDF page 31 of the 1985 kit is the 1985
model-year revision and is not used for 1986.
