# Chevrolet Suburban 1977 exterior-color source audit

## Decision

The 1977 GM Heritage Vehicle Information Kit contains a complete Suburban
“Interior and Exterior Color Availability Chart” at PDF page 6, printed
Suburban Page 6. The chart is dated August 9, 1976. The public timeline publishes
the 15 boldface primary exterior colors from that chart.

The chart also lists secondary colors for two-tone combinations. Those are not
additional solid-color offerings and are not converted into timeline rows. PDF
page 7, printed Suburban Page 7, separately identifies six Special Two-Tone ZY3
and Exterior Decor Package ZY5 combinations.

## Withheld two-tone evidence

PDF page 7 lists the following primary and secondary pairs for ZY3 and ZY5:

| Primary code and color | Secondary code and color | Hood stripe |
|---|---|---|
| 25 Mariner Blue (Dark) | 20 Lite Blue (Light) | Dark Blue |
| 43 Seamist Green (Light) | 12 Frost White | Medium Green |
| 53 Colonial Yellow | 60 Santa Fe Tan | Gold |
| 70 Cardinal Red (Medium) | 12 Frost White | Dark Red |
| 71 Metallic Red (Dark) | 76 Mahogany | Light Red |
| 81 Cordova Brown (Dark) | 60 Santa Fe Tan | Bronze |

These combinations will require a separate two-tone data model that preserves
primary and secondary placement. They are not evidence for additional solid
colors.

- PDF pages 6 and 7 were rendered and visually reviewed at full-page resolution.
- No adjacent-year color was inferred.
