# Chevrolet Suburban 1983 exterior-color source audit

## Decision

The official 1983 GM Heritage Vehicle Information Kit contains a complete C/K
Suburban “Interior and Exterior Color Availability Chart” at PDF page 27,
printed C/K Suburban Page H. The chart is dated February 1983. The public
solid-color timeline publishes its ten boldface primary exterior colors.

The dedicated 1982 Suburban PDF is a nine-page 6.2-liter diesel-equipment
supplement and contains no exterior-color chart. No 1982 color is inferred from
the 1981 or 1983 chart. A separate full 1982 governing source remains a research
gap.

## Separate two-tone evidence

The lower section of PDF page 27 lists eight ZY5 Exterior Decor Package paint
schemes. The chart expressly says those interior and exterior combinations are
the only ZY5 combinations available.

| Primary code and color | Secondary code and color | Stripe colors |
|---|---|---|
| 19 Black, Midnight | 17 Silver (Metallic) | Vermillion/Light Slate |
| 21 Blue, Light (Metallic) | 17 Silver (Metallic) | Dark Blue/Light Slate |
| 29 Blue, Midnight | 17 Silver (Metallic) | Vermillion/Light Slate |
| 63 Bronze, Light (Metallic) | 59 Almond | Dark Brown/Almond |
| 70 Red, Carmine | 59 Almond | Dark Carmine/Almond |
| 70 Red, Carmine | 17 Silver (Metallic) | Vermillion/Light Slate |
| 17 Silver (Metallic) | 19 Black, Midnight | Vermillion/Black |
| 17 Silver (Metallic) | 29 Blue, Midnight | Light Blue (Metallic)/Dark Blue |

These combinations remain a separate paint-scheme evidence class. Component
colors are not inferred to be standalone offerings from this section.
