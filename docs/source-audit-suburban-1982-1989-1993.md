# Result

This audit closes all three bounded regular Chevrolet Suburban palette gaps without borrowing colors from an adjacent year, another model, a two-tone paint-scheme diagram, or a third-party paint database.

| Model year | Disposition | Direct model-year evidence | Qualification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1982 | Resolved | GM-authored 1982 Chevrolet Suburban sales brochure, PDF p. 10, `Specifications-Suburban` color strip | Ten source-literal names. The brochure does not print numeric paint codes or configuration restrictions. |
| 1989 | Resolved | GM-authored 1989 Chevrolet Suburban sales brochure, PDF p. 6, `Exterior Colors` swatches and `Interior & Exterior Color Availability` matrix | Ten source-literal names. The brochure does not print numeric paint codes. |
| 1993 | Resolved with carrier qualification | GM-authored `1993 Chevy Blazers/Suburban` sales brochure, `Exterior Colors` foldout, Suburban column, preserved listing image 14 of 16 | Ten source-literal names and numeric codes. The public carrier is a marketplace listing's complete 16-image set, not an official GM PDF or a page-perfect archival scan. The underlying photographed page is unambiguously Chevrolet-authored and model-specific. |

`Resolved` here means that a GM-authored source directly maps a regular color list to Chevrolet Suburban for the exact model year. It does not mean that the audit found every special-order, fleet, or SEO color. Those require separate direct evidence.

## Search endpoints and rejected leads

All searches were performed on 2026-07-21.

- XR793 Chevrolet 1980-1989 index: https://xr793.com/chevrolet-1980-1989 . It supplied the controlling 1982 and 1989 Suburban brochure scans.
- XR793 Chevrolet 1990-1999 index: https://xr793.com/chevrolet-1990-1999 . It listed the 1993 full-line and truck brochures but no dedicated 1993 Suburban PDF.
- Auto Catalog Archive Chevrolet index: https://autocatalogarchive.com/chevrolet/ . It lists a limited set of Suburban brochures and no 1993 US Blazers/Suburban scan. A direct request for its 1982 Suburban PDF returned HTTP 403 during this audit, so no incomplete response was saved as a source.
- The Old Car Manual Project GM Trucks and Vans index: https://www.oldcarbrochures.com/static/NA/GM%20Trucks%20and%20Vans/dirindex.html . Its displayed collection ends at 1988, so it did not supply a 1993 brochure.
- 1993 Chevrolet Suburban and Blazer owner's manual: https://www.carmanual.org/OwnersManual/chevrolet/1993_chevrolet_suburban.pdf . The manual explains the service-parts identification label but does not publish the model-wide color palette.
- GM fleet order-guide site: https://www.gmfleetorderguide.com/ . No publicly accessible 1993 historical Suburban order guide was found.
- Marketplace listing for a 1993 Chevrolet Suburban ordering-information flyer: https://www.ebay.com/itm/365059140959 . Direct capture returned HTTP 403 and the two listing images were not needed after the full Poshmark photo set supplied the model-specific GM brochure page.
- Dealer-literature description for the 1993 Chevrolet Truck Graphic Information Booklet: https://www.faxonautoliterature.com/1993-Chevrolet-Truck-Graphic-Booklet-Original-Dealer-Album-Supplement . The page describes a 28-page original Chevrolet booklet but does not expose a complete scan.
- Third-party paint and specification pages, including AutomotiveTouchup, PaintScratch, Edmunds, CarSpecs, and retailer fitment catalogs, surfaced in searches but were excluded from evidentiary use. Their lists were not used to fill or verify a row.

# No-inference controls

- The 1981, 1983, 1988, 1990, 1992, and 1994 palettes were not used as substitutes.
- The 1989 and 1993 ZY2, ZY3, and ZY4 diagrams were not treated as standalone palette lists.
- Tahoe, Blazer, S-Blazer, GMC Suburban, pickup, and passenger-car colors were not copied into a Chevrolet Suburban year.
- No numeric code was supplied for 1982 or 1989 because the controlling model-specific brochures do not print one.
- The 1993 codes are transcribed only because the Suburban column prints them beside each color.
- Special-order and fleet colors remain outside this regular-palette closure unless separately and directly documented for Chevrolet Suburban in the exact year.
